About

Two bakers, one oven, one very old sourdough starter.

Moss & Moon started in 2019 as a single deck oven in a converted garage. Owners Ines and Tobias bake overnight, the way their grandmothers did, and open the doors at first light. The name comes from the two things they never run out of: moss on the north wall of the old building, and the moon they watch rise over the proofing racks every night.

Today the bakery still keeps the same hours and the same starter — now over eleven years old — and every recipe on the menu has been tested, argued over, and approved by the whole crew before it ever reaches a customer.
