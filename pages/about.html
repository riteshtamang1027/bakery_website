About Us

Moss & Moon began as a single deck oven in a converted garage. What started as a weekend hobby between two friends turned into a neighborhood fixture — a place where the lights are on before sunrise and the smell of butter and toasted grain reaches the sidewalk before the doors even open.

M

How We Started

Owners Ines and Tobias bake overnight, the way their grandmothers did, resting dough for a full day before it ever sees the oven. The name comes from the two things they never run out of: moss on the north wall of the old building, and the moon they watch rise over the proofing racks every night.

Every recipe on the menu has been tested, argued over, and approved by the whole crew before it reaches a customer — and the sourdough starter that opened the doors in 2019 is still the one used today, now well over eleven years old.
